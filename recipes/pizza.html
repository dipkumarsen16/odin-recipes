Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish consisting of a flatbread base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of ingredients such as vegetables, meats, or herbs. Traditionally baked in a wood-fired oven, it combines a crisp crust with gooey melted cheese and savory toppings, creating a balance of textures and flavors that has made it popular worldwide.

Beyond its taste, pizza is a cultural icon—adaptable to local flavors and enjoyed in countless styles, from thin-crust Neapolitan to deep-dish Chicago. It’s often shared among friends and family, symbolizing comfort and togetherness, whether eaten at a casual gathering or a festive celebration.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato pizza sauce
  • Grated mozzarella cheese
  • Capsicum (sliced)
  • Onions (sliced)
  • Sweet corn (optional)
  • Oregano
  • Chili flakes
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven or pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Place the pizza base on a tray or tawa.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce evenly on the base.
  4. Sprinkle grated mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add sliced onions, capsicum, and any other toppings you like.
  6. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes for seasoning.
  7. Drizzle a little olive oil on top (optional).
  8. Bake or cook until the cheese melts and the crust becomes slightly golden.
  9. Slice and serve hot.
